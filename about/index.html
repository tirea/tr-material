About Tirea Radio

This is the first Internet streaming radio station created by Avatar fans for Avatar fans. Here you can hear all the community-made music (most of which has Na'vi-language lyrics) and occasionally some other audio such as spoken Na'vi by Paul Frommer, creator of the Na'vi Language for Avatar. Anyone may apply to join in our Live Broadcasts. A Broadcast schedule can be found on the Schedule Page.
